About This Project

Exploring the intersection of education and technology through a robust Management System.

The Vision

This Student Management System is a comprehensive solution engineered for the Full Stack Lab (Experiment 12). It serves as a practical implementation of academic theories, bringing together frontend interactivity and backend stability.

The primary purpose of this system is to streamline the administrative overhead of educational institutions by providing a centralized, secure, and lightning-fast digital ledger for student records.
